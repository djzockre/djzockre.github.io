Senior Graphic Designer

Fluxo is seeking a Senior Graphic Designer to be located in its San Fransisco office.

  • San Fransisco, US
  • Full time
  • Design
Apply Now

Summary

Fluxo is looking for a smart, creative Senior Graphic Designer to help generate, execute and produce great ideas. Must be capable of working dynamically within multidisciplinary teams, have a strong interest in branding and corporate identity, and be familiar with creating brand identity work. You should have exceptional creative talent, be proactive and dedicated with a great sense of humor.

Responsibilities

  • Ideate and create impactful and memorable visual experiences for a dynamic spectrum of clients
  • Help generate/execute/produce great ideas and design executions
  • Work dynamically with multidisciplinary teams
  • Execute and produce design communications for diverse media
  • Collaborate and coordinate with all disciplines within Urge strategy, verbal, client management and implementation to fulfill client needs

Requirements

  • Bachelors degree in graphic design (or related field)
  • 5+ years of design experience; branding agency experience a plus
  • Experience as an illustrator in a marketing agency or in-house creative department.
  • Strong design, verbal, organizational and time management skills
  • Experienced in all current design programs
  • Must include a portfolio to be considered
